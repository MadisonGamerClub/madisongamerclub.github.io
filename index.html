Welcome!

We are the Madison Gamer Club and we're happy to have you! We're a club of adult gamers who have interests all across the spectrum including board games, tabletop RPGs, fighting, and everything in between. This group is for anyone who is looking to make friendships that extend outside of club activities and maybe occasionally do things outside of your wheelhouse.

  • Meeting times: Every 1st Saturday and 3rd Sunday
  • Location: Local gaming venues around Madison, WI (see the FAQ)
  • Discord: https://discord.gg/AFSWNJWfHt. This is where all specifics about upcoming events will be. Note that there is a screening form upon joining, see the FAQ.
  • GMail: madisongamerclub
