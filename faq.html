Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where do you meet specifically and when?

For specific details on our events, join our Discord: https://discord.gg/AFSWNJWfHt. We do not list details on this site or our posters because we want to have some control over who shows up to meetings (as in not just drive bys that aren't actually interested in what we have to offer).

Q: Why is there a screening form on the Discord?

Following on from the previous section, this is effectively just a simple vibe check. The more you sincerely say about your interests and what you are looking to get out of the club the better! This is not supposed to be anything intense like an interview; if you take this guidance you will more than likely get in.

Q: What types of events do you run?

What types of events we have varies from month to month. Here are some example of past events to give a taste:

  • Dungeons & Dragons one-shot for beginners
  • Arcade game high score contests
  • Pokemon Go at the capitol
  • and many more ...
